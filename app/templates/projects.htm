Projects

VEEP partners with the local community to promote engineering growth and leadership! Our projects are designed to be technically challenging, requiring a commitment of 7-10 hours per week. If you are a student please click on one of the projects below to apply; if you are a not-for-profit please click on the "Contact Us" tab or click the link here: Contact Us

This year all projects will have a mandatory Year End Project Showcase. It is our strong belief that students should be rewarded for their hard work, and we have a few events in the pipeline to make the extra effort of creating a presentation for all to see worthwhile. Please keep track of the Events page for more updates.

VEEP Projects

These are the traditional VEEP projects, which require students to be able to take initiative and perform difficult architectural decisions. There will be some guidance through our Technical Project Managers, but ultimately design decisions lie with the student team. Students that apply for traditional VEEP projects typically have extensive experience in the project's field, or have shown lightning fast learning speeds through prior endeavours.

VEEP-X Projects

VEEP-X is a program that we started in the fall semester of 2017. Realizing that there was a gap for students just starting their engineering career, we produced a program with projects that are a little less technically demanding and a little more well defined. Students applying for these projects have little to no experience in engineering projects -- these projects are their first performance in a professional environment. If you have no engineering experience, no worries! We are looking to develop the next generation of engineering students, so these projects will be heavily focused on learning opportunities, rather than project outcomes.
